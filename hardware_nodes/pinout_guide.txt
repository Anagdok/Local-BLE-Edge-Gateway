=========================================
HARDWARE PINOUT GUIDE - SMART HOME SYSTEM
=========================================

NODE 1: CLIMATE (Board: ESP32-C3)
-----------------------------------------
Role: Temperature, Humidity, and Light sensing.
Power: 3.3V and GND from ESP32 to all sensors.

1. AHT10 Sensor (I2C):
   - SDA  -> Pin 8
   - SCL  -> Pin 9

2. Analog Light Sensor (LDR):
   - OUT / AO -> Pin 2


NODE 2: AUDIO & LED (Board: ESP32-32D / WROOM)
-----------------------------------------
Role: Non-blocking I2S noise monitoring and LED control.
Power: 3.3V and GND from ESP32 to microphone.

1. INMP441 Microphone (I2S):
   - L/R  -> GND (Sets to Left Channel)
   - WS   -> Pin 25
   - SCK  -> Pin 26
   - SD   -> Pin 27

2. Status LED:
   - built in led on pin 2
